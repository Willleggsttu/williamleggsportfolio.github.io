Chicago-Atlanta Versus Video

A project filmed and edited by me to compare the rap scene of Atlance vs Chicago.

Overview

This project focused on creating a video to compare music from different states while keeping supporters engaged.

My Role

I handled video editing, visual storytelling, pacing, and overall creative direction.

Process

I started by reviewing raw footage, selecting key moments, and building a storyboard. I then edited the video, added transitions, and sound design.

Results

The final video delivers a polished, music video esque experience that aligns with the theme of our versus.

Reflection

This project improved my ability to balance storytelling with creativity and taught me how to create impactful visuals under time constraints.
